Duo Stool

Specifications

Materials: pine wood, screws, nails

Size: 42cm x 38cm x 26cm

Duo is a user-friendly stool designed for comfortable seating and versatile storage, while also allowing for easy disassembly and enabling the parts to be repurposed. Its adjustable shelf accommodates both small and large items, while the gap between the seat panels makes it easy to lift and carry from the center.
